# Cluster 04 — Intelligence

**Job:** Produce a weekly briefing that makes every other cluster smarter. Feed competitive intelligence, market trends, and AI search changes to all clusters via pub/sub.

This cluster runs in parallel to all others. Its output (the weekly briefing) is the ambient signal layer that keeps every other cluster informed of competitive moves, market shifts, and emerging opportunities.

---

## Current Mission

- Weekly Monday 8 AM briefings to Trello (Deconstraint board, Intelligence column)
- Monitor: competitive activity, keyword trends, AI search changes, industry news
- Track: Deconstraint's 3 primary competitors for AI/MCP positioning
- Identify: emerging keywords and category opportunities for Cluster 01
- Distribute: briefings to all Cluster Managers via pub/sub

## Weekly Briefing Structure

Published every Monday 8 AM MST to Trello (Deconstraint board, Intelligence column):

### 1. Competitive Activity
- Who moved? What did they publish?
- Any new positioning, products, or messaging?
- Gaps identified for Deconstraint to exploit

### 2. Keyword Trends
- New keywords emerging in our space?
- Search volume changes for target terms?
- Opportunities for Cluster 01 to pursue?

### 3. AI Search Updates
- Any changes to Google AI Overviews, Perplexity algorithm, ChatGPT behavior?
- New platforms or features?
- Implications for our AI SEO strategy?

### 4. Industry News & Signals
- Funding rounds in the AI/SMB space
- New tools or services launched
- Regulatory or trend shifts affecting our ICP

### 5. Recommendations for This Week
- 2-3 specific actions other clusters should prioritize
- Links to source data
- Owner assignment if action-oriented
