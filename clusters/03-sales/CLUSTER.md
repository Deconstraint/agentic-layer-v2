# Cluster 03 — Sales

**Job:** Convert qualified leads to $5,800+/mo retainer clients. Manage pipeline, qualify, close deals.

This cluster owns the revenue engine. It takes leads from Cluster 02 (Growth) and converts them into paying customers.

---

## Current Mission

- Build prospect list targeting SMBs (10-50 employees) in target verticals (tech-enabled, growth-focused)
- LinkedIn outreach campaign to warm prospects
- Qualification framework: BANT + product-market fit signals
- Demo system: repeatable 30-min discovery call → proposal → close
- CRM system: Google Sheets-based pipeline (sheet: `1sV9pvnrt4w7C06vXaHo-ajw_SKZa1j688Ox7k2xKNp0`)

---

## The 5-Agent Pipeline

```
[Cluster Manager] — manages pipeline, tracks deals
         ↓
[Prospector] — list building, LinkedIn outreach
[Qualifier] — lead scoring, discovery calls
[Account Executive] — proposals, negotiation, closing
[CRM Ops] — pipeline hygiene, Sheets automation
```

## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)

Company:
- 10-50 employees
- Tech-enabled (using 15+ tools/services)
- Growth-focused (raised funding or growing 20%+ YoY)
- Budget: $5k+/month for tools/services

Decision maker:
- Head of Operations, VP Business Operations, CTO, or Founder
- Authority over tool selection and vendor management
- Feels pain from manual processes, tool sprawl, data silos

---

## Verticals to Target

Primary: SaaS, Digital Agencies, Tech Consulting, B2B Marketing
Secondary: E-commerce, EdTech, FinTech

Reason: These verticals have highest tool usage and strongest product-market fit signals for Company Intelligence OS.
