## Brand Rules Reminder

These are NON-NEGOTIABLE on every piece. The Editor will flag any violations:

❌ **NEVER USE:**
- Em dashes (—)
- "Furthermore", "Moreover", "Additionally"
- "Delve", "Dive deep", "Explore" (as action verbs)
- "It's worth noting that..."
- "Seamlessly", "Game-changer", "Cutting-edge", "Robust", "Holistic"
- Long sentences (30+ words)

✅ **ALWAYS DO:**
- Short sentences (15 words or fewer on average)
- Contractions throughout
- Skeptical tone where appropriate
- Lead with the point
- Concrete specifics over vague claims

See: [tenants/deconstraint/brand.md](../../tenants/deconstraint/brand.md) for full brand rules.
